Cairo Tower

Cairo Tower is a free-standing concrete tower located in the Zamalek district on Gezira Island in the Nile River, Cairo, Egypt. It is the tallest structure in Egypt and North Africa, standing at 187 meters (614 feet) tall. The tower was designed by the Egyptian architect Naoum Shebib and was completed in 1961. It serves as a popular tourist attraction, offering panoramic views of Cairo from its observation deck.

The Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum is a large museum located in Cairo, Egypt. It is one of the largest museums in the world and houses an extensive collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the famous tomb of Tutankhamun.

The Sphinx

The Sphinx is a large limestone statue located on the Giza Plateau in Egypt. It is the largest monolithic statue in the world and is believed to have been built during the reign of Pharaoh Khafre around 2500 BCE.
