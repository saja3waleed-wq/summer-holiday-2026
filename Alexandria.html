Alexandria Library

The Alexandria Library is a historic library located in Alexandria, Egypt. It houses a vast collection of books and manuscripts, making it a significant cultural institution in the region.

Qaitbay Castle

Qaitbay Castle is a medieval fortification located in Alexandria, Egypt. It was built in the 15th century and served as a defensive structure against Ottoman invasions.
